Personality
Development PROGRAM

John Robert Powers offers a full range of holistic courses for all age groups specializing in the soft skills that are critical to success in life, but are often not taught in school.

We focus on the four pillars of personal development:
  • Building internal confidence and resilience
  • Training for public speaking and effective presentations
  • Coaching on presenting the best image of yourself
  • Developing the social skills to thrive in personal, academic, or business environment

With a century-long legacy of excellence, our programs are renowned for their transformative impact, enabling individuals to develop a strong sense of self and succeed in today's dynamic world.

ENGLISH LEARNING
PROGRAM

In JRP, our objective goes beyond just achieving English proficiency. We aim for our students to cultivate internal confidence first as the foundation, empowering them to navigate the world and express themselves with ease.

We do it by integrating the following key features in our English Learning Program:
  • Assessing one's proficiency level to tailor the program to the student's needs
  • Building a solid foundation in the English structure
  • From thought to speech: transforming the student from being a hesitant English speaker to someone comfortable with various communications styles, and can embody confidence, charm & persuasiveness
  • Switching from conversational English to Business English & Business Writing
  • Recognizing & incorporating cultural/situational contexts in communication

Our program is also structured to offer interactive and task-based learning to ensure immediate practical applications through constant conversations and fun activities to expand the level of comfort.
